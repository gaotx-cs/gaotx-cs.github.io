I have been an Assistant Professor in the [School of Computing](https://www.cdm.depaul.edu/academics/Pages/School-of-Computing.aspx) at [DePaul University](https://www.depaul.edu/Pages/default.aspx) since July 2024.

Before joining DePaul, I completed my Ph.D. in [Computer Science](https://www.cs.iastate.edu/) and a co-major in [Applied Mathematics](https://math.iastate.edu/) from [Iowa State University](https://www.iastate.edu/), co-advised by [Dr. Hongyang Gao](https://faculty.sites.iastate.edu/hygao/), [Dr. Hailiang Liu](https://faculty.sites.iastate.edu/hliu/), and [Dr. Jia (Kevin) Liu](https://kevinliu-osu.github.io/). I also hold a bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from [Yantai University](https://www.ytu.edu.cn/) and spent a summer conducting research at [Argonne National Laboratory](https://www.anl.gov/mcs).

My research focuses on **deep learning theory**, **generative AI**, and **graph representation learning**. I enjoy exploring fundamental problems that mysteriously arise from real-world applications and offering insightful guidelines to advance practice.
