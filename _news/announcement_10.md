I’m pleased to share that I have been appointed as a Visiting Research Member at the Institute for [Mathematical and Statistical Innovation (IMSI)](https://www.imsi.institute/) at the **University of Chicago** for Spring 2026.

During this visit, I will participate in IMSI’s long program on [Theoretical Advances in Reinforcement Learning and Control](https://www.imsi.institute/activities/theoretical-advances-in-reinforcement-learning-and-control/), engaging with researchers on theoretical foundations of reinforcement learning, optimization, and learning dynamics.

I’m looking forward to a stimulating Spring in Chicago and the many discussions and collaborations ahead.
